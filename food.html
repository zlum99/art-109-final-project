Dining

Tuskers Grill

Open daily, Tuskers Grill is located just inside Flamingo Plaza. A variety of food is served with outdoor seating available. We encourage guests to order their food and head to the meadow for a picnic.

The Landing Cafe

The Landing Café offers chef-inspired cuisine and breathtaking views. The menu features a variety of food, and the observation deck provides panoramic views of six Bay Area counties.

Savanna's Edge

Located across from the African Veldt, home to Giraffe and Eland, stop by for a snack or cold drink, and marvel at these amazing animals.

Toon Zoo

Have a beer, help wildlife – simple as that! Town Zoo is a light, crisp, refreshing Kolsch Style Ale created in partnership with Ale Industries. Each purchase aids Oakland Zoo's efforts to support our partners at ARCAS in Guatemala.

Safari Cafe

The Safari Café is located in Adventure Landing. The menu offers fresh cheesy pizza, soft served ice cream, salty pretzels and cold beverages to enjoy before or after a ride on the Oakland Zoo train.
